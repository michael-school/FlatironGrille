Who We Are

Located in Jackson, TN, we are commited to provided the highest quality dining experience possible. Over the years, we have been honored to receive the Jackson Sun's prestigious Readers' Choice Award for best business lunch and five times for fine dining.

The Premier Venue for Fine Dining and Entertainment

We offer an amazing selection of chef prepared entrées, our menu features choice steaks, chops, fresh seafood, pasta, entrée salads, burgers and sandwiches. You can also relax and enjoy the best drinks in town at our bar.

Flatiron Grille also hosts performances from many musical artists for your enjoyment.
